about

"Traditional music is the authentic heart of Bhutanese culture"

Bhutanese music artists may not be widely known on the global stage compared to artists from some other countries. However, Bhutan has a rich cultural heritage, and traditional Bhutanese music plays a significant role in their society. Traditional songs often revolve around themes of love, nature, and religious teachings.
